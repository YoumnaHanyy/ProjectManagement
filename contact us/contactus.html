Contact Us

If you have a question about a specific pet or policies at a shelter, please contact them directly. Asking Petfinder will delay your search for a pet, since each shelter manages its own pet list and information.

ARE YOU LOOKING FOR A NEW PET?

Our platform hosts a large, searchable database of pets in need of adoption from thousands of shelters and rescue groups. If you’re searching for a new companion, start by using our advanced Pet Search feature.

We update our listings daily, so check back often. Many shelters provide detailed info about temperament, medical history, and more to help you find the perfect match.

ARE YOU A SHELTER/PLACEMENT GROUP THAT NEEDS ASSISTANCE?

We’re dedicated to supporting shelters that list their pets on our site. If you’re having issues registering, posting pets, or managing listings, please contact our shelter support team.

We can guide you through technical setup, creating effective pet profiles, and best practices for increasing visibility.

DO YOU WANT MORE INFORMATION ABOUT A PET YOU'VE SEEN?

The organization responsible for the pet is the best source of detailed info. They can confirm availability, share insights on behavior, and detail adoption requirements.

Look for the shelter’s contact info in the pet’s listing and reach out to them directly for the most accurate updates.

ARE YOU TRYING TO FIND A HOME FOR A PET?

Rehoming a pet can be challenging. Check out our guide on “Finding a Home for a Pet” for tips on profiles, meet-and-greets, and more.

Local shelters and rescue groups may also offer courtesy postings or foster-to-adopt programs. Reach out for additional support.

DO YOU NEED MORE INFORMATION ON ADOPTION POLICIES?

Adoption policies vary widely among organizations. Some may require a home visit; others have flexible guidelines. Fees may also differ based on services like spay/neuter and vaccinations.

If you’re interested in a specific pet, contact the shelter or rescue group listed on that pet’s profile for detailed info.

ARE YOU HAVING TROUBLE ENTERING YOUR LOCATION?

Make sure you’re entering a valid ZIP (U.S.) or postal code (Canada). For Canadian codes, remember the space (e.g., A1B 2C3).

If the problem persists, clear your cache/cookies and try again. Otherwise, let us know, and we’ll investigate further.

WOULD YOU LIKE TO LINK TO PETFINDER?

We appreciate your interest! We offer embeddable widgets like the Pet List Widget and Featured Pet Module to showcase adoptable pets on your site.

By displaying these widgets, you help pets get more visibility and provide a convenient search tool for your visitors.

DO YOU HAVE CONCERNS REGARDING A PET'S HEALTH OR SAFETY?

Contact the shelter or rescue group responsible for the pet if you suspect any issues. They have direct info and can act accordingly.

In urgent situations where an animal is in immediate danger, please contact local animal control or law enforcement.
